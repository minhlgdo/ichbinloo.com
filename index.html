    About me

    My name is Minh Luong-Do Ngoc, a Vietnamese studying Computer Science and Engineering at Seoul National University, South Korea. Before going abroad, I also got admitted to Hanoi University of Science and Technology. I earned my High School diploma from Nguyen Hue High School for the Gifted, Hanoi, Vietnam.

    Although my academic background is natural science and technology-oriented, I have a strong interest in current social issues and social science subjects. In the long run, I want to conduct disciplinary research to improve the education quality, especially in social science education in K-12 schools in Vietnam. Also, I am interested in the robotics field. I have made two robots so far, and one of them was funded by my College of Engineering.

    When having free time, I write blog (mostly in Vietnamese) and make media productions on my public Facebook page and Youtube.
